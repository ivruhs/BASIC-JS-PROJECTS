Blackjack Game Rules

1. Objective of the Game

The goal of the game is to get a hand value as close to 21 as possible, without going over. If the total value of your cards exceeds 21, you lose automatically (bust). The player competes against the dealer, and the winner is the one who has the hand closest to 21.

2. Card Values

  • Ace (A) is worth 1.
  • Face cards (King, Queen, Jack) are worth 10.
  • Number cards (2-10) are worth their face value.

3. The Deal

Both the player and the dealer are dealt two cards at the beginning of the round. The player's cards are shown face-up. The dealer's cards are dealt with one face-up and one face-down card (the "hole" card).

4. Player's Turn

The player has the option to:

  • Hit: Take another card to increase the hand value.
  • Stand: Keep the current hand and end the player's turn.

The player can hit multiple times, but if the total value of the hand exceeds 21, the player busts and loses the round.

5. Dealer's Turn

After the player stands, the dealer reveals their hidden card (hole card). The dealer will always draw until their hand value is at least 17. If the dealer busts (goes over 21), the player wins automatically.

6. Winning the Game

The player wins if:

  • Their hand value is closer to 21 than the dealer’s hand.
  • The dealer busts (goes over 21).

The dealer wins if:

  • The dealer’s hand value is closer to 21 than the player’s hand.
  • The player busts (goes over 21).

If both the player and the dealer have the same hand value, the round results in a push (a tie), and the player’s bet is returned.

7. Blackjack

When the sum of cards of the player is equal to 21, we say it's a Blackjack.

8. Betting

The player must place a bet before the round begins. The minimum and maximum bet limits are defined. If the player wins, they receive the amount equal to their bet. If the player loses, they lose their bet amount. If the player ties with the dealer, their bet is returned.

9. Game Restart

Once a round is complete (win, loss, or push), the game can be restarted. The player can choose to place a new bet for the next round.
